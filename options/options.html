Winday Meet — Settings

Microphone

Allow the microphone to capture your voice (the "You" channel). Without it, only the participants' audio is recorded.

Transcription

Nova-3 transcribes in the language spoken. "Multilingual" auto-detects and handles calls that switch between languages; pick a specific language for the best accuracy on single-language calls.

Appearance

Panel display

Native mode uses the browser's side panel (it pushes the page aside). If your browser doesn't show it (Arc), choose "Embedded in the page".

Notion

Connect your Notion once. Winday creates a “Winday Meeting Notes” database in your workspace and sends every meeting note there automatically — no database ID to copy.
